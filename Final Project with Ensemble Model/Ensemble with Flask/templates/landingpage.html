Diagnostic of Cardiovascular Diseases

Chronic Heart Failure Detection

Computer-Aided Auscultation, Home-based CHF Monitor , Early Detection, Removes logistic burden

Chronic Heart Failure (CHF) is typically a long-term condition that gradually worsens over time. Also known as Congestive Heart Failure or heart failure, is an ongoing inability of the heart to pump enough blood through the body to ensure a sufficient supply of oxygen. CHF affects over 26 millions of people worldwide, and its incidence is increasing by 2% annually.

ABOUT Our Project

DESIGNER & DEVELOPER

Our aim is to detect whether the patient is having symptoms of CHF with the help of Heart Sounds.

The method combines classic Machine-Learning (ML) and end-to-end Deep Learning (DL). The classic ML learns from expert features, and the DL learns from a spectro-temporal representation of the signal.
